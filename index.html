About me

PhD candidate at the Rosenstiel School using climate models to study climate variability and change. Interest in and past work on climate and energy policy.

Climate

Current work evaluating the influence of model complexity on global sources of climate variability. past work on the role of model resolution on temperature variability. part of Amy Clement's research group.

Energy

Past research on carbon free energy (nuclear, wind, and solar). see the Nuclear Fuel Cycle Cost Calculator.

Policy

MS in Environmental Science and Policy from the University of Chicago Harris School of Public Policy. more fun webtools: RPS Calculator and webDICE.
